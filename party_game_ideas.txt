5 Party Game Ideas

1. Reverse Charades
One person guesses while the rest of their team acts out a word or phrase together. Set a one-minute timer and award one point for each correct answer.

2. Mystery Sound Challenge
Players take turns creating a sound with an everyday object behind a screen. Everyone else guesses what object made the sound; the first correct guess earns a point.

3. Two Truths and a Lie: Party Edition
Each guest shares two true stories and one invented story about themselves. The group votes on which story is the lie, earning a point for every correct vote.

4. Minute-to-Win-It Relay
Create a series of quick challenges, such as stacking cups, moving candy with chopsticks, or keeping a balloon airborne. Teams race to complete every challenge.

5. Secret Mission
Give each guest a private, silly mission at the start of the party, such as getting someone to say a certain word or starting a group high-five. Players reveal their missions at the end and earn a point for each one completed unnoticed.
